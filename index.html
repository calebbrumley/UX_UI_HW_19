Hello World!

My name is Caleb Brumley, a UX/UI designer who gets excited by excellent design and emotionally compelling storytelling.

My UX Skills

User Research

User research is the foundation of quality, user-centered design. Understanding the user and their needs is a crucial first-step in any project.
Tools: User Interviews, User Persona, Surveys

Empathy

I design with the end user in mind. This means constantly putting myself in their shoes, embracing their emotional journey as my own.
Tools: Storyboard, User Journey Map, Empathy Diagram

Qualitative Interviews

My approach to interviewing users is to establish specific goals in order to better understand a users’ pain points, routine, and how ultimately they use the product.

My Work
